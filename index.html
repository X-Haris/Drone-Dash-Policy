Privacy Policy for Drone Dash

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Introduction

Drone Dash ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed by Haris Abduloski. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Drone Dash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection to function and does not track users in any way.

Third-Party Services

Drone Dash does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Drone Dash does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
