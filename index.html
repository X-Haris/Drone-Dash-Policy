Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

About Drone Dash

Drone Dash is a mobile game developed and published by Haris Abduloski. This privacy policy describes how information is handled when you use the app. By using Drone Dash, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Drone Dash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or files.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Internet & Network

Drone Dash does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not make any network requests and does not communicate with any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Drone Dash does not integrate any third-party services, including analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media SDKs.

Local Data Storage

Game progress, scores, and user preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted externally and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Drone Dash does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Permissions

Drone Dash may request the vibration permission to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. No other device permissions are requested or required.

Data Security

Since Drone Dash does not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security risks associated with using the app. All game data remains on your device under the protection of your device's own security measures.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you wish to remove all locally stored game data, simply uninstall the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
