Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

About Drone Dash

Drone Dash is a mobile game developed and published by Haris Abduloski. This privacy policy describes how information is handled when you use the app. By using Drone Dash, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Drone Dash itself does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or files.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Third-party services integrated into the app (listed below) may collect certain data as described in their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Drone Dash integrates the following third-party services, which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to show rewarded video ads in the app. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID and other device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your consent choices. See Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps for details.
  • Google Play Games Services: Used for leaderboards. When you sign in with Google Play Games, Google may collect your display name, profile picture, and game scores. This data is handled by Google according to their Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Used to understand app performance and detect crashes. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data such as app version, device type, and crash reports. No personally identifiable information is collected through Firebase. See Google's Firebase Privacy page for details.

Advertising & Your Consent

Drone Dash displays rewarded video ads powered by Google AdMob. When you launch the app for the first time, you may be shown a consent form (depending on your region) asking whether you agree to personalized advertising.

  • If you consent: AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show ads tailored to your interests.
  • If you decline: AdMob will still show ads, but they will not be personalized.
  • Changing your choice: You can review or change your consent at any time via Settings → Privacy in the app, or by resetting your device's Advertising ID in your Android settings under Settings → Privacy → Ads.

US State Privacy Rights

If you are a resident of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, or another US state with applicable privacy laws, you have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for advertising purposes.

To exercise this right, you can:

  • Decline personalized ads when shown the consent form on first launch.
  • Reset or opt out of ad personalization via your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Contact us at haris.abduloski@gmail.com to request restricted data processing.

Local Data Storage

Game progress, high scores, coins, upgrade levels, and user preferences (such as sound and vibration settings) are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted externally and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Leaderboard Data

When you play Drone Dash with Google Play Games signed in, your highest altitude score is submitted to the Google Play Games leaderboard. This score is associated with your Google Play Games profile and is visible to other players according to your Google Play Games privacy settings.

Children's Privacy

Drone Dash does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The third-party services used (Google AdMob, Google Play Games, Firebase) are operated by Google and subject to their own age-related policies.

Permissions

Drone Dash may request the following device permissions:

  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay.
  • Internet access: Required for ads, Google Play Games leaderboards, and Firebase analytics.

Data Security

All local game data remains on your device under the protection of your device's own security measures. Data transmitted to third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play Games, Firebase) is handled securely according to their respective security practices.

Your Rights

Since we do not directly collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete on our end. For data held by Google through AdMob, Play Games, or Firebase, please refer to your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com. To remove all locally stored game data, simply uninstall the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
